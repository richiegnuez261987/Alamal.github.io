About Us

Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting is fast gaining recognition in the field of the construction business. It delivers contracting services for general construction of residential and commercial buildings, nonresidential buildings including schools, hospitals, hotels, etc., and pre-fabricated buildings on sites providers in diverse industries in K.S.A.

With increasing projects in the whole of Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting can accommodate all kinds of projects from Civil, MEP, Infrastructure, Communication, General Contracting.

Our employees and management work hand in hand to achieve success and to give our clients a better if not the best result when it comes to construction.

Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting is equipped with the knowledge to execute projects with milestones.

We, at Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting, would like to give you all the best Engineering and Construction projects and we deliver results with success. It is in our mind that everyone projects small or big matters the same and dealt with equal importance and achieves milestones.

MISSION

Our mission is to responsibly provide quality workmanship in the field of construction and our services through innovation, learning, and operational excellence while sustaining maximum value for our stakeholders.
To provide Construction services of the highest quality, conforming to the International regulations and standards.
To ensure that the Investor & Share Holders get the best returns from our business operations.
To provide utmost satisfaction and great pride to all stakeholders who are associated with our company, products and services.

VISION

Our Vision is to be the best in the industry and to ensure the long-term success of our business by contributing economically, environmentally, and socially to the communities in which we operate.
To become one of the leading Construction Conglomerates of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and thereby contributing to the economy of the Kingdom.
To develop an organization structure which will attract, retain, develop and motivate high caliber staff dedicated to the implementation of our vision and mission.

CORE VALUES

We Serve……. We Run……... We adapt……We stay……. We commit humbly.


HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY

Health, safety, and environment policy at Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting ensures the safeguard, health, and welfare of its employees and conserving the environment. All employees have an important responsibility towards their own safety and their fellow employees. Appropriate training, supervision, and information shall be provided to encourage and enable employees to carry out their duty safely and properly. By adhering to the company safety policy, the common objective of total safety will be achieved. In doing so, this will reflect individual and corporate professionalism which is the keystone of our existence as a leading industrial force of Al Amal Architectural Foundation for General Contracting that safety is synonymous with quality. Since we are committed to excellence it follows that minimizing the risk of injury to people and damage to the equipment and products is inseparable from our overall company objectives.
